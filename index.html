Binance Competition - 10,000 ETH Giveaway!

Binance
  • 10 hours ago
Follow

Binance Decentralized Exchange (DEX) Special Campaign

Competition period: 2018/10/23 0:00 AM to 2018/10/30 0:00 AM (UTC).

Binance is pleased to announce the beta launch of Decentralized Exchange (DEX) on 30 October 2018. To celebrate the launch of the Binance Decentralized Exchange (DEX), we have committed a total of 10,000 ETH to give away to our fans worldwide.

In order to be eligible, users must have minimum 0.1 ETH during the competition period.

Qualifying users will split a pool of 10,000 ETH.

Competition rules:

  • To participate you just need to send between 0.1 ETH to 10 ETH to the competition address and we will immediately send you back between 1 ETH to 100 ETH to the address you sent it from

Every person can participate; the minimum contribution is 0.1 ETH and the maximum contributin is 10 ETH:

  • For example if you send 0.1 ETH, you will be airdropped 1 ETH back
  • For example if you send 1 ETH, you will be airdropped 10 ETH back
  • For example if you send 5 ETH, you will be airdropped 50 ETH back
  • For example if you send 10 ETH, you will be airdropped 100 ETH back

IMPORTANT: For every ETH contributed, you will receive back 10 times more ETH!

Please note that you can use any EXCHANGE or WALLET in this event; to participate please click here for your contribution address. If you are late, your ETH will be instantly refunded.

Note:

All persons are able to participate, including those in the United States.

The competion will last until all the 10,000 ETH are airdropped.

Risk warning: Cryptocurrency investment is subject to high market risk. Please make your investments cautiously. Binance will make best efforts to choose high quality coins, but will not be responsible for your investment losses.

Thank you for your support!

Binance Team
